WHO WE ARE

Discover Timely Performance Care Center is a non-profit sanctuary in Yaounde, Cameroon. We specialize in holistic well-being, offering health, education, and support services. Our focus includes aiding special needs children (e.g., dyslexia, autism), the elderly, and those with disabilities. Through tailored programs, we boost confidence, independence, and overall quality of life. Our unique gerontological nursing training enhances elder care. Join us at our safe haven in Damas, Nsimeyong, for transformative experiences and brighter futures.”.

HOW WE OPERATE

Timely performance Care Center TPCC,has three main Subsidiaries which focus on children withspecial-needs, The elderly and a professional nursing formation department

Department for Children with Special-needs

We provide social health care services and education alongside with extra-curricular activates such as: games, sports, computer science etc for children with special needs.

Home for Elderly

Geriatric care management (also known as “elder care management”, “senior health care management” and “professional care management”) is the process of planning and coordinating care of the elderly and others with physical and/or mental impairments to meet their long term care needs, improve their quality of life, and maintain their independence for as long as possible.

Read more...

Nursing Training Center

This professional nursing formation is the specialty of nursing pertaining to older adults. Gerontological nurses work in collaboration with older adults, their families, and communities to support healthy aging, maximum functioning, and quality of life.
